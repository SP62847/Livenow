FAQ

Answers to common questions about our fun car racing games.

Are these games free?

Yes, all games on Velocity Apex are completely free to play! They are made by fans of racing games for everyone to enjoy.

How do I save my high score?

Your high scores are automatically saved in your browser. If you clear your browser's data, your score will be reset, so be careful!

Why isn't the game loading?

Please make sure you have a good internet connection and are using a modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari. Try refreshing the page!

How often are new games or tracks added?

We are always working on new and exciting content! Check back often for new cars, tracks, and even brand new games to play.

Is an account required?

No account is needed to play our games. Just visit the site and start racing instantly!

How can I give you an idea for a game?

We'd love to hear from you! Please use the "Get in Touch" contact form on our home page to send us your awesome ideas.

Are you part of a big game company?

No, Velocity Apex is an independent, fan-made website and is not affiliated with any major game studios.

What can I play these games on?

Our games are built to work on most modern computers, tablets, and mobile phones. Just open your browser and you're ready to go!
