Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-22

Overview

Velocity Apex ("we", "us", "our") is a website with fun car racing games for kids. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and games (the "Service"). We do not require you to sign up for an account and we collect as little data as possible.

Information We Handle

  • Game Data: High scores and unlocked cars are stored in your browser’s local storage. This data stays on your computer or tablet and is not sent to us.
  • Contact Information: If you or your parent chooses to contact us via our contact form, we receive the information you send, such as your name and email address, so we can write back to you.

Data Security

We are committed to keeping our players safe. Since we do not store personal data on our servers, your game data (like high scores) is as secure as your own device. We encourage you to have a parent help you with any security concerns.

Children's Privacy

Our games are made for kids and families to enjoy. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. Our website might use your browser's `sessionStorage` or `localStorage` to save your game progress and high scores. This is for the game to work and is cleared if you clear your browser's data.

Your Choices

  • You can ask a parent to help you clear your browser's site data at any time to remove all locally stored scores and unlocked cars.
  • You can use your browser's private or incognito mode to play without saving any data on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will let you know of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. We recommend checking this page with a parent every once in a while.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please ask a parent to help you use the contact form on our Home page.
